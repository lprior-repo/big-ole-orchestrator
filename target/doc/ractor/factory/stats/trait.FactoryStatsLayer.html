fn factory_ping_received(&self, factory: &str, sent: Instant)

Called when a factory ping has been received, marking the duration between when it was sent and now.

Measures ping latency on the factory

Source

fn worker_ping_received(&self, factory: &str, elapsed: Duration)

Called when a worker replies to a ping request from the factory, measuring the duration between the time the ping was sent and the factory processed the ping response.

Measures worker “free” latency and this metric relates to identification of “stuck” or slow workers.

fn job_completed(&self, factory: &str, options: &JobOptions)

Called when a job is completed to report factory processing time, worker processing time, total processing time, and job count

From these metrics you can derive

  1. Time in factory’s queue = factory_job_processing_latency_usec - worker_job_processing_latency_usec
  2. Time in worker’s queue + being processed by worker = worker_job_processing_latency_usec
  3. Total time since submission = job_processing_latency_usec
