Routing protocols for Factories

Structs§

CustomRouting
Factory will dispatch to workers based on a custom hash function.
KeyPersistentRouting
Factory will select worker by hashing the job’s key. Workers will have jobs placed into their incoming message queue’s
QueuerRouting
Factory will dispatch job to first available worker. Factory will maintain shared internal queue of messages
RoundRobinRouting
Factory will dispatch to the next worker in order.
StickyQueuerRouting
Factory will dispatch jobs to a worker that is processing the same key (if any). Factory will maintain shared internal queue of messages.
